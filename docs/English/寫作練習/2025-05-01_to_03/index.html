寫作練習｜2025-05-01 ~ 05-03

練習方式：給定 5 個指定單字，用這些字寫一篇短文

2025-05-01｜Gratitude#

指定單字： delicious、stranger、grateful、weather、performance

文章：

Grateful today. It was a beautiful day with good weather. My friend came to watch my show with her little daughter, and I ate some delicious food. I also met some strangers who were listening while I was singing. Now it's time for bed. Thanks for everything.

回饋：

  • "a beautiful day with good weather" 兩個概念略重疊，可改 "The weather was beautiful and the day felt complete."
  • 整體自然清晰，單字融入流暢

2025-05-02｜Inspiration and Challenges#

指定單字： adventure、challenge、courage、explore、experience

文章：

You never know what will happen today. Every day is like an adventure, and you need a lot of courage to face challenges. It may be uncomfortable, but it's important. Life requires us to keep exploring to help ourselves grow. Finally, you'll find that all experiences are worth it.

回饋：

  • "to keep exploring to help ourselves grow" 可簡化為 "to keep exploring and growing"
  • 哲學感強，句子有變化，結尾收得好

2025-05-03｜Bobo the Cat 🐱#

指定單字： gather、glance、whisper、spill、smooth

文章：

Let's talk about my cats. My first cat is named Bobo. He's a super smart cat. He always waits for me to come home, and he waits for us to go to sleep together. He always sleeps on my head, my body or near my face – we're really close. Sometimes, I gather pieces of my memories and ask myself, "Who do I love the most?" The answer is definitely Bobo. I can't imagine my life without him. Did he ever spill anything? Absolutely. But like I said, he's super smart, and he understands everything I tell him. Every night, when I look at him, I whisper many stories hidden in my mind. And through it all, Bobo taught me how to love. Life isn't always smooth – it's full of pain. But love is the only answer.

回饋：

  • 這篇最好，完全不像在「用指定單字寫作」，像真的在說故事
  • "I gather pieces of my memories" 和 "I whisper many stories hidden in my mind" 有詩意
  • 整體情感自然，是三篇中最強的
