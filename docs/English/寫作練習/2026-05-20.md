**指定單字：** solitude、away、bright、dawn、still

**文章：**

When my grandma leave this world away, suddly my whole life was so still.
I don't know what should I do? or what should I feel?Even  I'm a solitude man, still can feel what named is call heartache.
Sky still bright when the dawn comes.And I will never forget my grandma.

**回饋（第一版）：**

整體印象：真摯的情感，能感受到失去的重量。

優點：「Sky still bright when the dawn comes」這句很美，黎明的光還在，但人不在了——這種對比自然流露出悲傷，不用刻意說出來。

建議：
1. 時態要統一用過去式：`leave` → `left`，`is call` → `was called`，整篇在回憶奶奶，動詞要一致。
2. `suddly` 拼錯了 → `suddenly`

補充：「離開這世界」英文慣用 pass away，`my grandma passed away` 更地道。

---

**修正版（第二版）：**

When my grandma passed away, suddenly my whole life was so still.
I don't know what should I do? or what should I feel?Even  I'm a solitude man, still can feel what named was called heartache.
Sky still bright when the dawn comes.And I will never forget my grandma.

**回饋（第二版）：**

`passed away`、`suddenly`、`was called` 都修好了，進步明顯。

剩餘兩點：
1. 「Sky still bright」缺動詞 → `The sky is still bright` 或 `The sky was still bright`
2. 「what named was called heartache」仍有點卡 → `something called heartache` 或直接說 `heartache`

---

**修正版（第三版）：**

When my grandma passed away, suddenly my whole life was so still.
I don't know what should I do? or what should I feel?Even  I'm a solitude man, still can feel what something called heartache.
The Sky still bright when the dawn comes.And I will never forget my grandma.

**回饋（第三版）：**

非常接近了！剩最後兩點：
1. 「what something called heartache」多了 what → `something called heartache`
2. 「The Sky still bright」缺動詞，Sky 也不用大寫 → `The sky is still bright` 或 `The sky was still bright`

---

**✅ 最終版（第四版）：**

When my grandma passed away, suddenly my whole life was so still.
I don't know what should I do? or what should I feel?Even  I'm a solitude man, still can feel  something called heartache.
The sky is still bright when the dawn comes.And I will never forget my grandma.

**回饋（最終版）：**

全部修好，通過！三個版本下來每次都有具體進步。

補充（下次參考）：`Even I'm a solitude man` → `Even though I'm a solitary man`（`solitude` 是名詞，形容詞要用 `solitary`；加 `even though` 語氣更完整）

---

**✅ 完成版（第五版）：**

When my grandma passed away, suddenly my whole life was so still.
I don't know what should I do? or what should I feel?Even though I'm a solitary man, I still can feel  something called heartache.
The sky is still bright when the dawn comes.And I will never forget my grandma.

**回饋（完成版）：**

全部修正完畢，✅ 通過。從第一版到完成版修掉了：時態（leave→passed away）、拼字（suddly→suddenly）、用詞（solitude→solitary）、動詞缺失（sky bright→sky is bright）、主詞缺失（still can→I still can）、地道說法（leave this world away→passed away）。每一版都有具體進步。
